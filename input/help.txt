HELP MENU
	LIST OF COMMANDS:
	help/h - Calls this menu
	info - Shows all info about your pigeon care
	show - show [uid] | Shows you a pigeon of your choice
	breed - breed [uid] [uid] | Allows you to breed two pigeons
	buy - gives you a random pigeon to buy
	sell - sell [uid] | allows you to sell a pigeon
	rename - rename [uid] [newName] | allows you to rename a pigeon, if only an r is used for the name a random name is chosen from pigeonNames.json
	pass - ends month
	quit/q - Ends the game
